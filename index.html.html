Who We Are

Building a Year-Round Event Economy

As a proud chapter of the National Association for Catering & Events (NACE), we are committed to elevating our local event community through meaningful education, dynamic networking, and strong professional partnerships.

San Luis Obispo County has long been a top wedding destination. As wedding demand evolves and our industry diversifies, NACE Central Coast is leading the charge to attract corporate events, expand member services, and stabilize year-round cash flow for our entire community from planners and florists to venues, caterers, and rental companies.

Who We Are at Our Core

Mission, Vision & Values

Everything we do at NACE Central Coast is grounded in these guiding principles. They shape how we show up for our members, our industry, and our community.

🎯
Our Mission

Why We Exist

To create an environment of trust and respect in the catering and event industry on the Central Coast by providing education, advocacy and networking opportunities for its members.

🔭
Our Vision

Where We're Going

To be the catalyst for exceptionalism and evolution for catering and event professionals on the Central Coast.

Why Membership Matters

Your Member Benefits

Joining NACE Central Coast opens doors to knowledge, connections, visibility, and a thriving professional community right here on the Central Coast.

🤝

Powerful Networking

Connect with top event professionals across every category planners, caterers, venues, florists, photographers, and more. Build the referral network that grows your business.

🎓

Industry Education

Attend exclusive educational programs and workshops led by industry experts. Stay ahead of trends, sharpen your skills, and earn continuing education credits.

🎉

Mixers & Events

From casual mixers to our spectacular annual Gala, NACE CC events are the most fun you'll have while building your business. Your people are in that room!

📣

Vendor Visibility

Get featured on the NACE Central Coast website, in member email blasts, and on social media. Put your brand in front of the people who refer business in this industry.

💼

National NACE Resources

As a NACE member, you gain access to national resources, the CPCE certification program, industry research, and a network of event professionals across the country.

🌟

Community & Support

This is a community that shows up for each other. Get genuine support, collaboration opportunities, and the kind of professional friendships that last a career.

Support the Industry

Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsoring NACE Central Coast is more than just exposure it's a strategic investment in the future of events on the Central Coast.

When you support NACE, you support the future of our industry. Your investment helps attract more business events to our region, expand the services offered by our members, and stabilize cash flow during traditionally slower months benefiting every corner of our event community.

Friend of NACE CC
$0 $799
For community members & small businesses who want to show support at any level.
  • Verbal recognition at an educational program
  • 1 complimentary ticket to a program or mixer
  • Logo on event's Ticket Tailor ticketing page
  • Donations accumulate toward higher tiers
Supporter
$800
Perfect for businesses looking to stay visible and involved.
  • 2 complimentary tickets to a program or mixer
  • Verbal recognition at one event of your choice
  • Logo on NACE CC website public & member pages
  • Social media thank-you post (non-dedicated)
Program Sponsor
$1,200
Align your brand directly with one of our educational programs or mixers.
  • Logo on all attendee name badges
  • Logo on event marketing & presentation slides
  • 2-minute spotlight during the event
  • Featured in NACE member email blast
  • Opportunity to distribute branded materials
  • Recognition during event raffle
Advocate
$1,600
For brands ready to boost their exposure and credibility. Includes all Supporter benefits, plus:
  • 4 complimentary tickets to a program + 4 to a mixer
  • Advertising placement in event materials
  • Verbal recognition during the sponsored event
  • Featured in NACE member email blast
  • Logo on "About Us" page under Vendor Partners
  • Featured Vendor Spotlight on the News page
Partner
$2,500
For organizations ready to lead the conversation. Includes all Advocate benefits, plus:
  • 5-minute speaking slot at 2 programs + 2 mixers
  • Option to showcase a short promotional video
  • Dedicated "NACE to Meet You!" email introduction
  • 1 dedicated social media post
  • Premier static logo placement on all materials
  • Formal recognition photo with NACE check
  • Opportunity to co-brand a future workshop
  • 2 Gala tickets included

We gladly accept both monetary and in-kind donations of equal value. Ready to sponsor or want to learn more?
